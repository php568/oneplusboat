Sophie Ainsworth and Sophie Weguelin win bronze at World Sailing Championships

Submitted by Charles Ainsworth on Wed, 21/11/2018 - 18:26

Sophie Ainsworth and Sophie Weguelin have followed up their European Championship bronze with bronze at the World Sailing Championships in Aarhus, Denmark. Another fantastic achievement for for the two of them, who have also been nominated for Pro Sailor of the Year at the British Yachting Awards 2018. Please show your support by casting your vote on the site!

If you feel inspired by the girls do check out our sailing clothing for al the best deals this winter.
